About

Company Overview

Kohler Co., founded in 1873 in Kohler, Wisconsin, is one of the world's oldest and most respected names in kitchen and bath design. With a mission rooted in "Gracious Living," Kohler has spent over 150 years crafting products that combine bold design with cutting-edge engineering — from luxury faucets and intelligent toilets to designer bathtubs and shower systems.

In India, Kohler has rapidly grown since its 2006 entry, establishing a strong presence in the luxury and premium bathroom segments. The company operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Jhagadia, Gujarat, and has a network of over 1,000 retail touchpoints including Kohler Experience Centers in Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Pune, Hyderabad, and Chennai.

Kohler India serves the residential, hospitality, and commercial segments with a portfolio spanning bathroom fixtures, kitchen sinks and faucets, shower systems, and smart home products. The brand is a preferred choice for India's top developers, luxury hotels, and high-end residential projects.

Timeline

Key Milestones

1873
John Michael Kohler founded the company in Sheboygan, Wisconsin. Created the first enameled cast-iron bathtub by heating a horse trough and applying enamel.
1967
Introduced the Bold Look of Kohler campaign, redefining bathroom fixtures as design objects. Pioneered colored fixtures beyond white.
2006
Entered the Indian market, establishing India headquarters in Pune. Opened first Kohler Experience Center in Mumbai, targeting the luxury and premium segments.
2015
Launched the Kohler Numi intelligent toilet in India — featuring touchless controls, heated seat, built-in bidet, ambient lighting, and Bluetooth speakers.
2024
Expanded to 1,000+ retail touchpoints across India. Launched Kohler Konnect smart home ecosystem, integrating voice-controlled faucets, mirrors, and shower systems.
