About Raheja Universal

Raheja Universal Ltd, earlier known as K Raheja Company, has completed development of over 4.95 million sq.ft. of real estate in the Mumbai Metropolitan Region across 40 projects. Raheja Universal is known for its execution, planning, monitoring and control. It was chosen as a "Business Super Brand" in 2008 and "Consumer Super Brand" in 2009-10. The company has the ability to identify land and develop projects with strong potential for capital appreciation, and has been in the real estate business for over four decades.

By creating and delivering properties that are state-of-the-art, Raheja Universal intends to impact the real estate sector in Mumbai positively. This Mumbai-based developer is dedicated to offering high-quality settings that improve the present and look forward to the future. The company seeks to make its presence known by generating high-quality real estate projects that provide excellent value for money and the highest possible level of customer happiness.

In the competitive field of real estate in Mumbai, Raheja Universal is a major participant and is trying its best to bring quality and consistency to the table. As a real estate developer in Mumbai, this company is helping many home buyers and real estate investors achieve their dreams of owning their own homes. Every house is constructed with care, putting an emphasis on quality, practical details, and excellent value for money.
