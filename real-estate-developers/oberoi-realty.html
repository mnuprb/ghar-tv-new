About the Developer

Oberoi Realty Limited is a publicly listed real estate developer (BSE: 533273, NSE: OBEROIRLTY) founded by Vikas Oberoi and based in Mumbai. The company is recognised for its sharp focus on design quality, branded retail collaborations and operationally excellent mixed-use destinations.

Its portfolio spans premium residential, large-format retail, hospitality and Grade-A commercial — anchored by landmark addresses in Goregaon, Andheri, Worli and Mulund. Oberoi Realty has consistently ranked among India's top-performing listed developers by sales velocity and balance sheet strength.

The company partners with global hospitality and design names — including Ritz-Carlton, Westin and St. Regis — to bring international standards of finish and service to Indian luxury living.
