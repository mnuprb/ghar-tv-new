What is Developer Mandate

End-to-end project marketing, embedded in editorial

Not display advertising. Not paid listings. A premium media partnership where your project is featured the way GHAR.tv covers any other landmark development.

FAQs

Common questions

Who is Developer Mandate for?

Developer Mandate is for India's premium and top-tier real estate developers — those building landmark residential, commercial, or township projects. We work with developers who are looking for premium media partnerships, not display advertising.

How is this different from listing on a property portal?

Property portals are search-and-listing platforms. Developer Mandate is a content partnership where your project is editorially featured the same way GHAR.tv covers any landmark development — with full storytelling, video, intelligence inclusion, and event presence.

What's the typical commitment length?

Project Mandates start at a 3-month minimum. Portfolio Mandates are 6 months. Enterprise mandates are 12-month engagements. Most developers extend after the first cycle once they see the pipeline impact.

Do you guarantee leads?

We deliver qualified buyer enquiries with project-specific intent. We don't guarantee a fixed number — we deliver volume that scales with the audience the editorial creates. Past mandates have delivered 200–1,200 qualified leads per quarter.

How quickly can a mandate go live?

Most mandates go live within 3–4 weeks of signing. Editorial planning, video production, and event inclusion are all kicked off in parallel after the discovery phase.

Can I see past examples?

Yes. After your discovery call, we share a curated reference deck with anonymised case studies and metrics from comparable developer engagements.
