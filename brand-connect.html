What is BrandConnect

Your brand belongs where decisions happen

BrandConnect is GHAR.tv's content partnership platform for allied businesses — brands that want access to India's high-intent property audience through native content, sponsored campaigns, and creator-driven marketing. This is not banner advertising, not a self-serve ad platform, and not display ads. It's content and partnerships.

BrandConnect is the third B2B offering in GHAR.tv's "For Business" trio — alongside Developer Mandate (for developers) and SuperPro (for brokers). It's specifically for businesses outside the developer/broker ecosystem. Campaigns are priced at ₹50K–5L/month, with 5 active brand partnerships already live.
