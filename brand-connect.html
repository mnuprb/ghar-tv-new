About BrandConnect

Content partnerships for allied businesses

BrandConnect is GHAR.tv's advertising and content partnership platform — the third B2B offering alongside Developer Mandate (for developers) and SuperPro (for brokers). It's specifically for allied businesses outside the developer/broker ecosystem. Not a self-serve ad platform. Not display advertising. Content and partnerships — with 5 active campaigns and pricing from ₹50K–5L/month.
