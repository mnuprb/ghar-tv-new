What is BrandConnect

Content partnerships for allied businesses

BrandConnect is GHAR.tv's content partnership platform — the third B2B offering alongside Developer Mandate and SuperPro. It's for allied businesses that want access to India's high-intent property audience.

Not a self-serve ad platform. Not display advertising. Not for developers or brokers. Content and partnerships — campaigns priced at ₹50K–5L/month.

The Audience

High-intent buyers, not casual browsers

Every piece of content GHAR.tv produces grows an audience that is actively researching property decisions — calculating EMIs, reading reports, comparing projects.

BrandConnect monetises this audience through content, not ads. Your brand earns attention inside Editorial, Intelligence, Design, Videos, and Events — across the full buying funnel.
